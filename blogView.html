Trump çılgın mı yoksa sadece gerizekalı mı?

Politika Eleştiri

Image by Zafer Cetin

You know, I have been trying to keep my Trump diatribes down to once a week or less, but now that he has emerged from his self-imposed exile and returned to the national stage with his campaign style rallies, it’s become impossible.

The man is like a high-speed car wreck. You know that you shouldn’t look because it’s so gruesome, but you can’t look away because it is so horrific.

So what about his latest car wreck is causing me to helplessly slow down and gawk?

He’s, shall we say, rewriting history. Not to make himself look better but because he is just plain ignorant. Or crazy. These days it’s becoming increasingly difficult to tell the difference.
